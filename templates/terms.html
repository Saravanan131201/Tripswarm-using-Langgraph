02

Service description

TripSwarm is an AI-powered travel planning application that uses a multi-agent architecture built on LangGraph to help users plan trips. The Service includes, but is not limited to:

  • Trip Planner — an AI agent pipeline (flight research, hotel recommendations, itinerary planning) that generates personalised travel plans in response to natural-language prompts.
  • Travel Q&A (RAG) — a retrieval-augmented generation system that answers travel-related questions using a hybrid semantic and keyword search over user-uploaded documents and a base knowledge corpus.
  • Document Management — the ability to upload, store, and delete personal travel documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT) used to enrich Q&A responses.
  • Saved Trips — persistent storage of AI-generated travel reports linked to your account.
  • Chat History — persistent conversation threads stored and associated with your account.

The Service is provided as a portfolio and showcase project. It is not a commercial travel agency, licensed financial adviser, or booking platform, and does not guarantee the accuracy, availability, or price of any flight, hotel, or travel service mentioned in AI-generated output.
