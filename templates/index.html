About TripSwarm

Everything Your Trip Needs,
Handled by Agents

Multi-Agent Swarm

Four specialized agents — Flight, Hotel, Itinerary, and Response — collaborate via LangGraph to build your travel plan end-to-end.

Agentic RAG

Upload PDFs, DOCX, or TXT travel docs. The RAG agent uses ChromaDB + BM25 hybrid search to give sourced, accurate answers.

Live Flight Data

Real-time flight information via AviationStack and Tavily — airlines, durations, routes and schedules surfaced instantly.

Hotel Recommendations

Budget, mid-range, and luxury hotel picks with nearby accommodations tailored to your destination and travel style.

Persistent Chat History

All your trip conversations are saved in PostgreSQL — pick up exactly where you left off, manage multiple threads.

AI Travel Reports

Download professionally formatted Markdown travel reports — complete itinerary, flights, hotels, tips, and highlights in one file.

How Agents Collaborate

The Swarm in Action

When you ask TripSwarm to plan a trip, it doesn't use a single AI. It orchestrates a graph of specialized agents via LangGraph, each handing off enriched context to the next — like a swarm of experts collaborating on your behalf.

Getting Started

Trip Planning in
4 Simple Steps

01

Sign in with Google

One click, no passwords. Your account is secured via Google OAuth 2.0 and a JWT session token is issued instantly.

02

Choose Your Mode

Head to Travel Plan to build a full trip, or Ask Q&A to get RAG-powered answers from your uploaded travel documents.

03

Describe Your Dream Trip

Type naturally — destination, duration, preferences. The AI swarm kicks in immediately and coordinates all four agents in parallel.

04

Get Your Travel Report

Receive a full itinerary with flights, hotels, day-by-day plans, food tips, and highlights — downloadable as a report.
